Enterprise AI Copilot Leave Policy (2026):
All employees are entitled to 25 days of paid time off per year, plus 10 public holidays.
Employees can carry over up to 5 days to the next calendar year.
Sick leave is unlimited with a doctor's note required after 3 consecutive days.
Parental leave is 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers and 8 weeks for secondary caregivers.
